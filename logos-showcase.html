Brand Identity

Logo Showcase

Two logo concepts for Ravi Chodagam — each shown on dark and light backgrounds. Gold accent color (#c4a060) holds on both.

Logo 1 Mark (ascending bars) + Wordmark — geometric, structured
Logo 2 Typographic — serif/sans contrast, no icon

Logo 1 — Mark + Wordmark

  • Four ascending bars: opacity fades upward to suggest peak trajectory
  • Thin gold hairline separates icon from text — structural, not decorative
  • Jost 500, tracked wide — matches the site's wordmark rhythm
  • Works as standalone mark (just the bars) at very small sizes
  • Tagline optional — drop at small scales

Logo 2 — Typographic

  • Cormorant Garamond italic — same font as site headlines; signals authority
  • Serif/sans contrast: "Ravi" (warm, humanist) vs "CHODAGAM" (precise, modern)
  • Vertical hairline between names creates visual pause — editorial feel
  • Horizontal rule + tagline anchor the nameplate to a profession
  • No icon needed — the typographic tension is the identity

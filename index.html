01  /  About

25 years.
One consistent focus.

I started in technology delivery — TCS, Singapore and Australia — learning how global services actually work at the ground level. Not the deck. The delivery.

That foundation shaped everything that came after.

Over 25 years, I moved through client-facing roles at Cognizant and Tech Mahindra, running portfolios, managing global pipelines, advising Fortune 500 CXOs. Each move added a layer: more complexity, more P&L responsibility, broader scope.

What I learned: most IT services firms grow to a point and stop. Not because the market stopped — because the internal engine wasn't built to go further. The portfolio drifts. The sales motion gets stale. New logos stop coming in.

That's the problem I know how to solve. For the past several years, I've run a business unit with full P&L ownership — spanning Insurance, Manufacturing, Retail & Logistics, Life Sciences, Media, and more. I rebuilt the service mix from legacy to Data, AI, and Cloud. Built a hunting engine. Opened new verticals. Integrated acquisitions. Now I work with firms who want to do the same — and need someone who's been in that seat.
